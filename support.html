Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play the game?

Tap the screen to start the game, then time your taps to hit the moving target at the top of the screen. When you successfully tap, multiple lightning bolts will strike altcoin bubbles simultaneously. Try to destroy as many bubbles as possible to increase your score!

How are scores calculated?

Your score increases based on the number of bubbles you destroy with lightning attacks. Consecutive successful taps will build a combo multiplier for higher scores.

The game is running slowly on my device

No2ndBest is designed to work on devices running iOS 15.0 or newer. If you're experiencing performance issues, try closing other apps running in the background to free up system resources.
